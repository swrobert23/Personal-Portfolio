About Me

How a Technical Recruiter Became a Web Coder Rockstar:

Scott Robertson's Unconventional Journey to Web Development

Hey there! I'm Scott Robertson, your friendly neighborhood Technical Recruiter turned Web Slinger - err, I mean, Web Developer!

After seven awesome years of diving headfirst into the tech talent pool, I found myself thinking, "Hey, I want to swim with these cool coders!" And so, here I am, doing the digital doggy paddle and loving every minute of it.

My techie transformation took place at the digital Hogwarts known as Nucamp Coding Bootcamp, and in the virtual dojo called Udemy. Here, I turned from a mere muggle to a pro, mastering magical spells - alright, alright, codes - in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript ES6, Bootstrap 5, Node.js, and React.js.

With my recruiter radar and dev dexterity, I craft web wonderlands that users rave about. My past as a recruiter lends an X-ray vision into the business world, and a knack for building Avengers-style dream teams. Plus, I'm all about bringing the party to the people, making sure my work caters to a diverse crowd.

As I step into this brave new world of web development, I'm stoked to use my mixtape of skills to rock the tech stage. Stay tuned for some epic web jams coming your way!
